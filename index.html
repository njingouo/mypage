Welcome to my website

I am a PhD candidate in quantitative and computational sociology at the University of Quebec at Montréal (UQAM). My academic journey spans data science, applied demography, disciplines thatunderpin my research in population health My work combines rigorous statistical methods with machine learning approaches to address pressing issues such as the role of gender inequalities and norms on child mortality.

In addition to my own research, I serve as a research assistant on several projects, collaborating with interdisciplinary teams across Canada. My broader interests include computational social science and public policy evaluation.
